Smart Dorm Finder Flow
Step 1

Browse Available Dorms

Browse

Start your journey by exploring dormitories that match your goals and lifestyle.

  • Filter by location, price range, and amenities
  • View high-quality photos of each dormitory
  • Check availability and room types
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your preferences
Step 2

View Detailed Information

Explore

Review complete details to compare options and make an informed choice.

  • Browse through multiple photos and virtual tours
  • Read descriptions of amenities and facilities
  • Check exact location on interactive maps
  • Read tenant reviews and ratings
  • View landlord profiles and verification status
  • Compare room types and included features
Step 3

Make Your Reservation

Reserve

When you find the right dorm, send your reservation request in minutes.

  • Select your preferred check-in and check-out dates
  • Choose from available room types
  • Review total cost and payment terms
  • Submit your reservation request instantly
  • Receive confirmation or rejection notifications
Step 4

Complete Payment

Pay

After landlord approval, complete payment to finalize your booking.

  • Receive payment instructions and deadline
  • Pay securely via GCash using the provided QR code
  • Upload your payment proof for verification
  • Get confirmation once payment is verified
  • Access booking details and receipt

Important: Complete payment within 24 hours of reservation approval to avoid automatic cancellation.

Step 5

Communicate with Landlords

Chat

Use built-in messaging to coordinate and stay updated in real time.

  • Send direct messages to landlords
  • Ask questions about the dormitory or policies
  • Share important documents or images
  • Discuss move-in details and arrangements
  • Get real-time notifications for new messages
  • Keep conversation history for reference
Step 6

Share Your Experience

Review

Help future tenants by posting accurate and useful feedback.

  • Rate your overall experience (1-5 stars)
  • Write detailed reviews about the dormitory
  • Share feedback about cleanliness, facilities, and location
  • Comment on landlord responsiveness
  • Support community transparency and trust
  • Earn reputation points for helpful reviews

Helpful Tips

Use these practical tips to avoid delays and secure the best dorm option.

Book Early: Popular dorms fill quickly, especially before semester opening.

Read Reviews: Learn from previous tenants before committing.

Ask Questions: Message landlords to clarify house rules and inclusions.

Verify Details: Prioritize verified landlords with complete documents.

Check Location: Consider distance to school and transportation access.

Pay on Time: Complete payment within 24 hours to secure your booking.
