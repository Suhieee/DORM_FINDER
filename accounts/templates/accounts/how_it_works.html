How It Works

Your step-by-step guide to finding and booking your perfect dorm

1
Browse

Browse Available Dorms

Start your journey by exploring our wide selection of dormitories. Use our advanced search and filter features to find dorms that match your preferences.

  • Filter by location, price range, and amenities
  • View high-quality photos of each dormitory
  • Check availability and room types
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your preferences
2
Explore

View Detailed Information

Get comprehensive information about each dormitory to make an informed decision.

  • Browse through multiple photos and virtual tours
  • Read detailed descriptions of amenities and facilities
  • Check the exact location on interactive maps
  • Read reviews and ratings from previous tenants
  • View landlord profiles and verification status
  • Compare different room types and their features
3
Reserve

Make Your Reservation

Found the perfect dorm? Secure your spot by making a reservation.

  • Select your preferred check-in and check-out dates
  • Choose from available room types
  • Review the total cost and payment terms
  • Submit your reservation request instantly
  • Receive confirmation or rejection notifications
4
Pay

Complete Payment

Once your reservation is approved by the landlord, complete the payment to confirm your booking.

  • Receive payment instructions and deadline
  • Pay securely via GCash using the provided QR code
  • Upload your payment proof for verification
  • Get instant confirmation once payment is verified
  • Access your booking details and receipt

Important: Complete your payment within 24 hours of reservation approval to avoid automatic cancellation.

5
Chat

Communicate with Landlords

Stay connected with your landlord through our built-in messaging system.

  • Send direct messages to landlords
  • Ask questions about the dormitory or policies
  • Share important documents or images
  • Discuss move-in details and arrangements
  • Get real-time notifications for new messages
  • Maintain conversation history for reference
6
Review

Share Your Experience

Help future tenants by sharing your honest feedback and experience.

  • Rate your overall experience (1-5 stars)
  • Write detailed reviews about the dormitory
  • Share feedback about cleanliness, facilities, and location
  • Comment on the landlord's responsiveness
  • Help maintain community transparency and trust
  • Earn reputation points for helpful reviews

Helpful Tips

Book Early: Popular dorms fill up quickly, especially before the start of the semester.

Read Reviews: Check what other tenants say about their experiences.

Ask Questions: Don't hesitate to message landlords for clarifications.

Verify Details: Look for verified landlords with complete documentation.

Check Location: Consider proximity to your school and transportation.

Pay on Time: Complete payment within 24 hours to secure your booking.
